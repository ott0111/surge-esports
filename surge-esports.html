Who We Are

Born to Compete.

Surge Esports is an upcoming competitive Fortnite organization with one goal: rise to the top. We're building something real — an org that takes player development, community, and competition as seriously as the game itself.

We're not here to follow the scene. We're here to push it. Every squad, every scrim, every tournament is a step toward the top of the leaderboard — and we don't stop until we get there.
